Created in 1978, Rummikub (pronounced “rummy-cube”) is definitely one of the most fun board games for families. Players take turns placing numbered tiles in sequential runs and groups of the same. Jokers add spice and interest, and the first player to use all their tiles wins. The game is simple enough for kids to get but engaging enough for adults.
